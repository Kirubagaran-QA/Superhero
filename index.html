Welcome to the Cricket Documentation Page

Cricket is a popular sport played between two teams of eleven players each. It is enjoyed by millions of fans worldwide.

Basics of Cricket

  • Objective: The main objective is to score more runs than the opposing team.
  • Players: Each team has 11 players, including batsmen, bowlers, and fielders.
  • Equipment: Cricket bat, ball, stumps, and protective gear.

Formats of Cricket

  1. Test Cricket: Played over five days with unlimited overs.
  2. One-Day Internationals (ODIs): Limited to 50 overs per side.
  3. T20 Cricket: A fast-paced format with 20 overs per side.

Key Terms

Run
A unit of scoring in cricket.
Wicket
The set of stumps and bails, or the dismissal of a batsman.
Over
A set of six legal deliveries bowled by a bowler.

Popular Cricket Tournaments

Some of the most famous cricket tournaments include:

  • ICC Cricket World Cup
  • Indian Premier League (IPL)
  • The Ashes
